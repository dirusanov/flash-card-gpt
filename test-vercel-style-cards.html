üß¨ Biology - DNA Structure

DNA is a double helix structure consisting of two antiparallel strands connected by hydrogen bonds between complementary base pairs. Adenine pairs with thymine, and guanine pairs with cytosine. This structure allows for accurate replication and storage of genetic information in all living organisms.

üíª Computer Science - Big O Notation

Big O notation describes the computational complexity of algorithms in terms of time and space. O(1) is constant time, O(log n) is logarithmic, O(n) is linear, O(n¬≤) is quadratic, and O(2^n) is exponential. This notation helps developers choose efficient algorithms for large-scale applications.

üåç Geography - Plate Tectonics

The theory of plate tectonics explains the movement of Earth's lithospheric plates over the asthenosphere. Convergent boundaries create mountains and trenches, divergent boundaries form new oceanic crust, and transform boundaries cause earthquakes. This theory unifies geology and explains continental drift.

üìö Literature - Stream of Consciousness

Stream of consciousness is a narrative technique that presents characters' thoughts and feelings as a continuous flow. Pioneered by writers like James Joyce and Virginia Woolf, this method captures the natural progression of human consciousness, including fragmented thoughts, memories, and associations.
